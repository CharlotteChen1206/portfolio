Kuo-an Hardware – Real Client Branding & Website Project

Website building with Wix, Logo design & brand identity, Wireframing & prototyping (Figma), Client communication & design iteration

Introduction

This was my 100-hour final term project, where I worked with a real client to create a complete branding and website solution. My client owns Kuo-an, a hardware store in Taiwan. I was responsible for designing a new logo, brand colors, mockups, and a website that aligned with their existing identity. I began by researching their current brand, creating logo thumbnails, mockups, and final assets. Then I built wireframes and prototypes, continually improving the design through feedback from instructors, classmates, and the client. The site was built with Wix for efficiency, and a CMS was used to organize and display the store’s large product collection effectively.

Research & Data

I analyzed Kuo-an’s existing visuals and communication tone to ensure the updated design preserved brand recognition while enhancing clarity and appeal. Ongoing discussions with the client helped shape the final look and structure of the site.
